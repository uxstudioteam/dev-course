Tiramisu Lasagne Tailwind

Classic Lasagne

  • Prep: 40 min
  • Cook: 1 hour
  • Serves: 6
  • Medium

Ingredients

  • 12 lasagne sheets
  • 500 g ground beef
  • 400 g crushed tomatoes
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 500 ml béchamel sauce
  • 250 g mozzarella, grated
  • 50 g parmesan, grated
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • Fresh basil (optional)
